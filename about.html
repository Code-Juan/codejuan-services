About CodeJuan

Building websites that work as hard as the businesses they represent

Hi, I'm Juan Contreras

I'm a software developer who specializes in building websites for small businesses. My background in game development and systems programming gives me a unique perspective — I care deeply about performance, clean code, and building things that actually work.

I started CodeJuan Web Services because I saw too many small businesses getting overcharged for mediocre websites, or stuck with template builders that all look the same. Every business is unique, and your website should reflect that.

My approach is simple: understand your business, build something fast and beautiful, and make sure it actually brings you customers. No bloated templates, no hidden fees, no confusing contracts. Just clean, professional websites that get results.

My Process

A clear, structured approach to every project

1. Discovery

We start with a free call to understand your business, goals, and what you need. I'll recommend the right plan and give you a clear timeline.

2. Design

I create a mockup based on your brand and feedback. You approve the direction before any code is written.

3. Build

I develop your site with clean, performant code. You get regular updates and can provide feedback throughout.

4. Launch

After final review, your site goes live. Domain, hosting, SSL — everything is set up and ready. 3 months of free maintenance starts now.

With ongoing maintenance, I keep your site fast, secure, and up to date. Monthly reports show you how your site is performing.
